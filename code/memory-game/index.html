How To Play

  1. The goal is to find cards with matching icons and match all of the cards in the deck.
  2. Click on the card to open and view the icon for that card. The timer starts once you click on the first card of a game.
  3. You can open two cards at a time. If the two cards match, they will remain open. Otherwise, they will flip back over.
  4. Each pair of opened cards counts as one move. Your star rating will decrease with the number of moves taken.
  5. Once all cards are matched, the game is won. You will see a pop-up that shows your Time, Star Rating, and Number of Moves made.
  6. You can use the "Play again" button on the pop-up to start a new game, or you can use the refresh icon on the game board.

You win!
